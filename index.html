💍 B. Jewelry

On jewelry, zakat is due on the following categories:

(1) Business merchandise (You sell jewelry)
(2) Jewelry stored for financial purposes (held as savings, held for appreciation)
(3) Any jewelry with the intention of profit

No Zakah is due on personal jewelry that is worn occasionally and not intended for profit. The jewelry must have been owned for the duration of the hawl.
